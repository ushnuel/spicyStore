ABOUT US

KEM'S MEALS AND SPICES is a registered company with registration number - CAC 2672916

We all at KEM'S MEALS AND SPICES are passionate about what we do. We love our customers; and more especially, we love healthy, spicy, natural and delicious meals. We specialize in mixing traditional, quality organic spices to help you prepare healthy, delicious and tasty meals.

At Kem's Meals and Spices, we beliveve that innovation is key. We have carefully formulated a blend of organic spices, each one being individually tailored to complement the underlying fresh ingredients.

Our Spice Mixes are created with great care and attention, hygienically produced using only the very best organic spices. We combine the exact quantities and selections of spices for each mix by hand, package everything ourselves and with very simple easy to follow instructions pass on the knowldge to you.

We pride ourselves on delivering great products. Each one of our mixes is enough to make a generous dish for atleast 5 people. They are totally Glutten free, mixture free, MSG free, preservatives free and vegetarian
